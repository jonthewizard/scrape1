FAQ

FAQ

FAQ

How does SmartLists work?

SmartLists automatically generate leads and run LinkedIn DM campaigns based on specific triggers, allowing you to continuously engage prospects without relying on static lists. This makes outreach streamlined and scalable.

Can I use ReactIn with multiple LinkedIn accounts?

Yes! ReactIn supports multiple LinkedIn accounts, enabling you to run outreach campaigns across different profiles, ensuring personalized and efficient engagement at scale.

How does ReactIn integrate with other tools?

ReactIn integrates seamlessly with popular tools like HubSpot, Calendly, PipeDrive, and more, allowing you to automate outreach while syncing with your existing workflows and systems.

Is ReactIn suitable for agencies?

Absolutely! ReactIn’s agency plan is specifically designed for teams managing 10+ accounts. It offers features like white-labeling, permissions management, and custom automations to scale LinkedIn outreach efficiently.

Do I need LinkedIn Premium to use ReactIn?

No, LinkedIn Premium is not required to use ReactIn, although it can help build trust on your profile. ReactIn works with both Premium and regular LinkedIn accounts.

Can I personalize and segment my LinkedIn messages using ReactIn?

Yes, ReactIn allows both personalization and segmentation to ensure your LinkedIn messages are highly relevant and engaging. Personalization: You can customize LinkedIn DMs using dynamic fields like {{FirstName}}, {{CompanyName}}, and other variables, making your outreach feel more tailored and human. Segmentation: ReactIn enables you to segment your leads based on various criteria such as job title, industry, company size, or previous engagement. This allows you to send targeted messages to different prospect groups, ensuring your outreach is not only personalized but also strategically focused for better results.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes! ReactIn offers a free plan with 50 credits per month, allowing you to start automating LinkedIn outreach without any upfront cost.

How easy is it to set up ReactIn?

ReactIn is designed to be user-friendly. No technical expertise is required. You simply copy and paste to integrate with LinkedIn and your favorite tools.

How does ReactIn help convert leads into customers?

ReactIn’s automation tools help nurture leads by sending timely and personalized messages based on interactions, allowing you to stay top of mind and drive conversions effectively.
